FAQ
How does subscription service work?
Subscription provides you with advanced functionality to speed up your search for an expert or customer. Are you interested in getting the best deals? Check out our "Prices" page and choose the offer that suits you best!
Is there any time limit for the starting subscription plan?
There is no limit to the validity of the free account.
Who is the audience for this site?
The platform brings together customers and performers in the metal, woodworking, and 3D printing industries. So, if you need to place an order related to these areas, you can submit it on our website and choose a contractor. If you offer services in these fields, you can register a company and get recurring orders.
Where will my parts be produced?
Once you place an order, the performers registered on our platform will offer individual proposals regarding the production of your parts. The best partners are specialists/workshops that have the necessary capabilities and readiness to do your job so that they can get started on your order immediately. What about shipping costs?
The shipping arrangements are made individually with your chosen performer. When placing an order, please specify any shipping preferences/requirements. Both you or the performer can pay for the delivery.
What models or drawings do I need to have to get an offer?
Our platform supports downloading drawings in popular formats (STEP | STEP | SLDPRT | IPT | 3D XML | PRT | SAT). No need to worry if you don't have a 3D model. You can send a PDF or any other type of file.
In which industries do you work?
Our platform covers specialists and production facilities for all industries requiring metal, wood and polymer parts, such as automotive, machinery, railroads, aerospace, energy and many other sectors.
What are the benefits of working with the platform?
Users of Oxese platform can be both material suppliers and companies that can fulfill orders placed by platform customers. In return, we provide win-win terms of cooperation that are discussed individually. Fill in the feedback form, and we will contact you.
